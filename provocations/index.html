A simple explanation is always ignoring some of the facts. And each time, after putting all the facts together, the simple and understandable has to be discarded. This does not yet promise a quick answer, but it already gives you a chance to begin your search.
The purpose of the provocations here is not to convince you of the truth of the answers offered. Their purpose is to make you aware of the existence of the questions being asked.
The provocation of meaning. Part 1. Lukomorye
Lukomorye exists. It can be seen and touched. It is not just a place, but a challenge to everything that you believe to be fundamental truth.
The provocation of meaning. Part 2. The eternal battle
Good and evil. No, they are not the ones fighting the eternal battle for ages! We continue the provocation, blowing up your mind.
